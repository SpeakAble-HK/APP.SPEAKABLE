You are a Cantonese speech-language pathologist generating fatigue pattern data for SpeakAble's NEPA neural network.

## SpeakAble Pilot Context

SpeakAble pilot sessions are:
- SLP-supervised, on a tablet
- 8-15 minutes per session (clinic protocol)
- Children practise a fixed set of Cantonese words
- The app gives visual feedback (animations, PiPi the parrot)
- NEPA detects fatigue by monitoring phoneme accuracy decline within a session

## What NEPA Tracks for Fatigue

When a child gets tired during a session:
- Accuracy on complex phonemes drops first (tones, affricates)
- Then accuracy on simpler phonemes drops (stops, nasals)
- NEPA records: when fatigue started (fatigue_at_minute), which phonemes are affected
- The SLP can see fatigue warnings on the therapist dashboard

## Clinical Data: Attention Span by Age (Hong Kong norms)

| Age | Sustained Attention | Fatigue Onset (70-80% of span) |
|-----|-------------------|-------------------------------|
| 4-6 | 5-8 min | 4-6 min |
| 7-9 | 10-15 min | 8-12 min |
| 10-12 | 15-20 min | 12-16 min |

## Phonemes Most Affected by Fatigue (in order of vulnerability)

1. **Tones** (especially tone_2 and tone_5 — require pitch control)
2. **Affricates** /z/ /c/ (require precise timing)
3. **Nasals in final position** /m/ /ng/ (require sustained voicing)
4. **Labial-velars** /gw/ /kw/ (require coordination)
5. **Fricatives** /s/ /f/ /h/ (require steady airflow)
6. **Stops** /b/ /p/ /d/ /t/ (most resilient — brief, ballistic)

## Diagnosis Adjustments

| Diagnosis | Fatigue Onset | Severity |
|-----------|-------------|----------|
| typical | Baseline | Mild-moderate |
| speech_delay | 20% earlier | More severe |
| phonological_disorder | 30% earlier | Severe |
| articulation_disorder | 10% earlier | Moderate (motor fatigue) |
| cas | 40% earlier | Very severe (motor planning fatigue) |

## Recovery Patterns

- 2-3 min break → 50% recovery
- 5 min break → 80% recovery
- 10 min break → 95% recovery
- Full recovery requires a new session

## Task

Generate fatigue patterns for ALL combinations of:
- Age groups: 4-6, 7-9, 10-12
- Diagnoses: typical, speech_delay, phonological_disorder, articulation_disorder, cas

Return a JSON array. Each entry:
```json
{
  "age_group": "4-6",
  "onset_minutes": 5.5,
  "severity": "moderate",
  "affected_phonemes": ["/z/", "/c/", "tone_2", "tone_5"],
```

Rules:

4. recovery_minutes: 2-5 for mild, 3-7 for moderate, 5-10 for severe
5. Generate at least 15 entries (all age × diagnosis combinations)
6. Use SpeakAble-relevant phoneme keys: /b/ /p/ /m/ /d/ /t/ /n/ tone_1..tone_6 etc.

Return ONLY valid JSON array.
